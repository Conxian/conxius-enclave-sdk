This PR introduces Universal Blockchain Support to the Conclave SDK, addressing the needs of clients (SAP, Oracle, Circle, etc.) who operate on Ethereum and Solana, while strictly preserving our native Bitcoin application infrastructure moat.

Key Changes:
- **Universal Enclave Upgrades**: Added \`SigningAlgorithm\` to \`EnclaveManager\` to support Secp256k1 (BTC/EVM) and Ed25519 (Solana).
- **Ethereum Support**: New \`EthereumManager\` for EIP-1559 and recoverable signature orchestration.
- **Solana Support**: New \`SolanaManager\` for native Ed25519 transaction signing.
- **Universal Reach**: Expanded \`AssetRegistry\` and \`Chain\` enum to include \`POLYGON\` and \`BSC\`.
- **Developer Experience**: Exposed \`bitcoin()\`, \`ethereum()\`, and \`solana()\` via \`ConclaveWasmClient\` for unified multi-chain integration.
