## Deterministic coverage

`protocol::rails::rail_proxy_tests::test_stale_and_future_reports_are_rejected` now verifies:

- exact `MAX_ATTESTATION_FUTURE_SKEW_SECS` is accepted;
- future skew plus one second is rejected;
- exact `MAX_ATTESTATION_AGE_SECS` is accepted;
- stale age plus one second is rejected.

No sleeps or boundary-dependent wall-clock sampling are used.
