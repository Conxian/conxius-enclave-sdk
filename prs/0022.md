# fix: gate mock CloudEnclave and remove mock timestamps

Hardens mainnet safety by preventing mock-only time/constants from leaking into default builds.

### Changes
- Gate `enclave::cloud::CloudEnclave` behind `cfg(test)` or the `mock-cloud-enclave` Cargo feature.
- Replace hard-coded timestamps (`1710000000`) with current Unix time for business attributions and enclave attestation reports.
- Validate business attribution expiration against current time (instead of a fixed epoch).
